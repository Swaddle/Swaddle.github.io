Kitaev model

Take the square lattice on the torus \(\mathbb{T}^2\), with edge set \(E\), \(V\) vertices, and \(P\) plaquettes. For each edge assign a Hilbert space \[\mathcal{H}_e \simeq \mathbb{C}^2 \simeq \underset{g \in \mathbb{Z}_2}{\oplus} \mathbb{C} \ket{g}.\] There is a total space on the lattice \[\mathcal{H} = \underset{e \in E}{\otimes} \mathcal{H}_e .\]
